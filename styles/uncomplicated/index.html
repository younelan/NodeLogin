Welcome

For some reason, I decided that I needed to do a very simple design. Something that would load fast and still look moderately good. I realize it is a little lacking in the color department, but with the stylesheet, you could really fashion any sort of scheme you wanted. Feeling like some puke green? Go nuts! Urine Yellow? Why not!? Anyways, I hope someone can find some use for this design. I would love it if you stopped my website and let me know what you used it for.
